Cocos2d-x
Project Details / Background
cocos2d-x is a widely adopted open-source 2D game engine, known for its lightweight architecture, cross-platform capabilities, and high performance on mobile and embedded platforms. It has been used by companies like Ubisoft and Zynga to build hundreds of commercial games. More detailed information can be found in the cocos2d-x GitHub repository.
As one of the main developers and maintainers, I led the engine’s roadmap planning and technical direction, from the earliest porting of cocos2d-iphone to full Metal backend support. I actively engaged with the global developer community—resolving issues, gathering feedback, and improving usability. I also drove external collaborations with partners such as Google and Microsoft, and coordinated closely with distributed teams across China and the U.S. to ensure smooth cross-regional development and delivery.
